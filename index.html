About Me

I am Gordon James Ramsey. In 1998, I opened my own restaurent in Chelsea, with the help of my father. In 2011, The Good Food Guide listed my Restaurant 'Gordon Ramsay' as the second best in the UK, only bettered by The Fat Duck in Bray, Berkshire. In July 2006, I won the Catey award for "Independent Restaurateur of the Year", becoming only the third person to have won three Catey awards. In September 2006, I was named as the most influential person in the UK hospitality industry in the annual Caterersearch 100 list, published by Caterer and Hotelkeeper magazine.

Panzanella Salad

This Panzanella salad is perfect for barbecues, picnics, or party food buffets and it's also healthy for your body. It’s easy to prepare ahead and the ideal way to use up stale bread. Try serving with some top-quality Parma ham or Proscuitto for a meaty twist.

Tarragon Potato Salad

One of the best side dishes all along summer ins tarragon potato salad. This recipe for creamy Lemon Tarragon Potato Salad is just as simple, with a little more zippy, herb flavor! It is tasty as well as healthy.

Smoked Salt Steak Rub

This steak rub recipe can be adapted to fit your own preferences and works well on chicken and pork too. Try adding a little smoked paprika to the rub or some dried herbs. It’s best to marinate the steak the night before so that the salt can properly infuse and tenderise the meat.
